DESCRIPTION 

 E_SPECTRAS
 Functions for generating electron number-fluxes with
 time-dispersions for auroral precipitation that varies rapidly
 with time.
 
 Files
 Ie_E_with_LET             - electron spectra with low energy tail (energetic e^-)
 IE_FLICKERINGGAUSS_FLUX   - Gaussian with low-energy-tail, harmonic modulation
 IE_FLICKERINGGAUSS_FLUXS  - Gaussian with low-energy-tail, square-wave modulation
 IE_FLICKERINGGAUSS_FLUX2  - Gaussian with low-energy-tail, harmonic modulation
 Ie_PnL_flickering         - Peticolas and Lummerzheim flickering spectra
 Ie_PnL_flickering1Pulse   - single square-wave pulse of P-n-L flickering spectra
 Ie_PnL_flickeringS        - On-off modulated P-n-L flickering spectra
 Ie_PnL_flickeringH        - harmonically modulated P-n-L flickering spectra
 Ie_PnL_flickeringHtapered - harmonically modulated P-n-L flickering spectra, tapered flux-fall-off at low-energies
 Ie_PnL_flickeringStapered - On-off modulated P-n-L flickering spectra, tapered flux-fall-off at low-energies
 Ie_smooth4                - electron spectra smoothly varying in time
